Bruschetta

A classic Italian starter with toasted bread, tomatoes, and basil.

Ingredients:

  • Baguette slices
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic

Steps:

  • Toast the baguette slices.
  • Mix chopped tomatoes, garlic, basil, and olive oil.
  • Top the toast with tomato mixture and serve.
