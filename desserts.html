Fruit Tart

Fresh and colorful fruit tart with custard.

Ingredients:

  • Tart crust
  • Custard
  • Assorted fruits
  • Apricot glaze

Steps:

  • Fill baked tart shell with custard.
  • Arrange sliced fruits on top.
  • Brush with apricot glaze for shine.
